About Harris Ranch

Harris Ranch Master Association is located at the east side of Boise, Idaho, and is a non-gated subdivision. The Master Association is made up of multiple sub-communities. The neighborhood is tucked between the rolling Boise foothills and the free-flowing Boise River. There are two deep, open-water ponds and wetlands which attract and enhance the area's wildlife. In addition, the ponds provide a source of irrigation water for the HOA's common areas and home landscaping, as well as serve as part of the storm-water system. Trails provide residents an opportunity for a casual stroll and an up-close way to observe wildlife and enjoy natural habitat.
